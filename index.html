Epicodus Course Track Advisor

Epicodus offers five different tracks of study for students to choose from. Each track focuses on different languages, and each language occupies a particular place in the tech development world. The language each student should pursue depends on that student's goals for their future employment.

Offered tracks

  • Ruby/Rails
  • PHP/Drupal
  • Java/Android
  • CSS/Design
  • C#/.NET
